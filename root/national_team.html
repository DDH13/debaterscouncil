WSDC Process


The Sri Lankan National Debating Team's selection process is a thorough, multi-stage approach. It begins by forming a National Pool, including top speakers from local tournaments and passionate applicants. This pool undergoes further selection, followed by intensive training. The final evaluation leads to the formation of the national team, representing the country with intellectual rigor and critical thinking. Subsequently, the selected team engages in focused training sessions while also participating in other international tournaments as part of their preparation for the World Schools Debating Championship
