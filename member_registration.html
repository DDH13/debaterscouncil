Member School Registration

Why Join the Debaters' Council?

  • Access to Competitions: Your school will be added to an emailing list that all other DC registered schools are required to invite when organizing tournaments. Additionally, your school will receive priority invitations to participate in these events over other schools
  • Discounted Registration Fees: Enjoy reduced fees for all Debaters' Council workshops and other activities
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with other debating clubs, coaches, and debaters across the region
  • Resource Sharing: More access to debate materials and coaching resources
  • Official Recognition: Gain official recognition and credibility for your school's debating program
  • Regular Updates: Stay informed about upcoming events, competitions, and announcements through our WhatsApp network
  • Community Support: Be part of a vibrant community dedicated to promoting debate and critical thinking

How to Register with the Debaters' Council?

    All you need to do is get the following requirements in order and submit the below Google form with the relevant details. Once your submission is received and the membership fee is processed, your school will officially become part of the Debaters' Council network. You should receive an email from us and your representative will then be added to our WhatsApp group for further communications

  • Formal Letter: We require a formal letter expressing your club's intent to join the Debaters' Council. This letter should be signed by the Teacher in charge of the debating club or another relevant authority within the school.
  • Annual Membership Fee: An annual membership fee of 4000 LKR must be paid. This fee supports the activities and initiatives facilitated by the Debaters' Council.
  • Contact Information: Kindly provide us with the name and contact number of a designated person for WhatsApp communication (ideally the team captain). This individual will serve as a primary point of contact for any quick updates, announcements, and coordination.
